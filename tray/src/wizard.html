Welcome to Meridian

Meridian watches what you do and keeps your project tickets up to date — automatically, on-device. Let's get a few things set up. It takes about a minute.

Nothing leaves your Mac unless you connect a tracker at the end.

Permissions

macOS privacy

Meridian needs two macOS permissions to see your activity. Open each one in System Settings and toggle Meridian on — this window updates the moment a grant takes effect.

Reads on-screen text to understand what you're working on.
